This example demonstrates the following aspects of the web component:

  • With data-avoid-no-region="off", the component considers the full original image as a region valid region to select. If the component's ratio and the image's ratio are very close, the component will decide it's best not to zoom in on any region.
  • With data-avoid-no-region="on", the component will always prefer to zoom in on a region rather than selecting the full original image.
  • Toggle the behavior by clicking the Avoid no region checkbox.
